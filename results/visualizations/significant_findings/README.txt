SIGNIFICANT FINDINGS: AVH- vs AVH+ Analysis
============================================

Figures in this folder show statistically significant results or large effect sizes.

Classification criteria:
- p < 0.05 (FDR-corrected where applicable), OR
- Cohen's d >= 0.8 (large effect)

Subfolders:
-----------
roi_effects/     - ROI raincloud plots: L_MTG and L_STS show large effects (d = 0.83-0.92)
                   in sentences_vs_reversed and words_vs_sentences contrasts.
brain_maps/      - Surface brain plots (fsaverage5): whole-brain AVH- vs AVH+ differences.
                   Summary plot highlights significant regions.
psyrats_correlation/ - PSYRATS (hallucination severity) vs activation in AVH+ group.
                       R_STG_posterior: r = 0.59, p = 0.003 (speech_vs_reversed).

Key result: Left temporal regions (L_MTG, L_STS) differentiate AVH- from AVH+
during speech comprehension; right STG correlates with hallucination severity.
