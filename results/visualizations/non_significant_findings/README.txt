NON-SIGNIFICANT FINDINGS: AVH- vs AVH+ Analysis
==============================================

Figures in this folder show results that did not reach statistical significance
(p >= 0.05 or below-chance classification).

Subfolders:
-----------
mvpa_classification/ - SVM classification (AVH- vs AVH+): accuracy at or near chance;
                       no contrast reached p < 0.05. Weight maps shown for completeness.
connectivity/        - ROI-to-ROI connectivity: after FDR correction, no connections
                       remain significant. Heatmaps and network plot retained.
laterality/          - Laterality indices: no significant group differences in
                       hemisphere dominance (all p_fdr >= 0.05).

These null results are informative: group differences appear focused in left
temporal ROIs rather than whole-brain patterns or connectivity.
