Key Findings Summary:
--------------------
1. L_MTG and L_STS show large effect sizes (d > 0.8) between AVH- and AVH+
2. AVH- shows greater left temporal activation during speech comprehension
3. Classification accuracy ~60% (not significant) - differences are distributed
4. 2 significant connectivity differences between groups
5. No significant laterality differences
